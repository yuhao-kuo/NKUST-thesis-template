### Setting author information

Author information covers the author, advisor, school, department, thesis title, and related data. The fields live in `thesisinfo.tex`.
One special part of the layout settings: National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology was formed on February 1, 2018 by merging three universities of science and technology (National Kaohsiung University of Applied Sciences, National Kaohsiung First University of Science and Technology, and National Kaohsiung Marine University). The title page sample in the official guidelines annotates the English names of the merged schools, so this template provides that setting.
